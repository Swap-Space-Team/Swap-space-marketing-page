Email Tracking

Delivery and engagement for transactional emails sent by the SwapSpace platform. Click any row to see its full lifecycle (sent → delivered → opened → clicked).

  • Clicks are the trustworthy signal. A click is a real human action — prefer it for engagement.
  • Delivered reliably means the email reached the mailbox.
  • Opens are an estimate, not a fact. Gmail and Apple Mail pre-fetch and cache the tracking pixel, so opens and open counts often reflect machine prefetches rather than a person reading. Don't build critical logic on them.
  • A recipient with no name isn't a registered SwapSpace user (e.g. internal or test sends). Untagged emails are grouped as Uncategorized.
