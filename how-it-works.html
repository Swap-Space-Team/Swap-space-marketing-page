How home swapping works on SwapSpace

Members-only home exchange community. List your home, connect with verified members worldwide, and travel without paying for accommodation. Homeowners and renters welcome.

Can I swap my home?

Choose how you travel

Every Swap Space member gets access to both swap types. Use whichever suits the trip.

Mutual Swap

Swap homes directly with another member

You and another member agree to stay in each other's homes. Dates can be simultaneous or at different times, whatever works for both of you. No money changes hands for accommodation.

Best for: Travellers who have a specific destination in mind and want to connect directly with a member there.

SwapCredit Swap

Host first, travel whenever you are ready

Earn SwapCredits by hosting a member in your home. Spend those credits to stay at any home in the Swap Space community, whenever suits you. No need to find a perfectly timed match.

Best for: Travellers who want flexibility over timing and destination.

Every new member starts with 7 SwapCredits, giving you 7 nights of travel before you need to host anyone.
