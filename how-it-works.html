Mutual Swap

with another member

Host first, travel

Best for: Travellers who want flexibility over timing and destination.

How to get started

SwapSpace is a members-only home exchange community where verified homeowners and renters swap homes to travel affordably and authentically.

Apply to become a member

Swap Space is members-only. Complete a short application so we can verify your home and confirm your place in the community. The application takes around 2 minutes and asks about your home, your travel style, and when you are available to host.

Become a member

List your home

Once accepted, create your listing. Add photos, a description of your space, and your available dates. A complete listing earns more swap requests and unlocks your welcome SwapCredits.

Become a member

Start your home swap journey

Search member listings by destination, dates, and home type. Every listing belongs to a verified member. Send a swap request for a mutual exchange, or use your SwapCredits to book a flexible stay at any available home.
