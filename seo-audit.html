Social crawlers and Google are reading the wrong canonical URL. This corrupts every link preview and splits ranking authority.

3
Homepage meta description is 500+ characters — Google ignores it
Google truncates at ~155 characters and rewrites the snippet from body copy when the description is too long. You lose all control over your search result appearance and click-through rate.
Current: "Swap Space is the simplest and trusted home exchange platform for travelers looking to swap homes securely and affordably. We connect verified swappers from around the world..." [3 full paragraphs] Target: ≤ 155 characters with primary keywords
Direct SERP CTR impact index.html Copy task
4
Schema.org structured data has a broken URL
The JSON-LD markup is registered with Google but points to a fake URL. This is an active signal that your structured data is untrustworthy, suppressing rich results.
{ "@context": "https://schema.org", "@type": "WebSite", "name": "...", "url": "https://url" ← broken }
Rich results eligibility index.html Dev task · 2 mins
5
No canonical tags on any page
Without canonicals, if the site is accessible at both swap-space.com and www.swap-space.com, Google splits ranking authority between duplicate versions. This is a silent ranking penalty that compounds over time.
