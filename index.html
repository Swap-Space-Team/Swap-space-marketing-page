Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions, we have answers.

Our mission is simple: Travel can be better and cheaper. SwapSpace is a home exchange platform that allows members to swap their homes with others worldwide, enabling affordable and immersive travel experiences.

You can swap homes in two ways: through mutual swaps, where you and another member stay in each other’s homes, or one-way swaps using SwapCredits you earn from hosting others in your home.

Absolutely. Renters are welcome to list their homes on SwapSpace, since swaps are about exchanging stays rather than money. It works much like hosting a friend while you’re away. To be safe, we suggest reviewing your tenancy agreement or checking with your landlord first.

SwapCredits are the currency of the SwapSpace community. You earn them by hosting other members in your home, and you can use them to book one-way swaps in destinations that inspire you. Every new member also receives 7 welcome SwapCredits after listing their home, so you can start travelling even before hosting your first guest.

Everyone in the SwapSpace community lists their home. Since SwapSpace is invite-only, the first step is to apply. Once you’re accepted, you’ll be able to create your home listing and start exploring beautiful spaces in destinations that inspire you.

Your safety matters. Every member verifies their identity with a government ID, so you know you’re connecting with real, trusted people. Once verified, you’ll get a badge on your profile that shows you’re ready to swap with confidence.

SwapSpace is completely free to use for now. You can apply, list your home, and start swapping without any cost. In the future, we’ll introduce a membership fee to support platform maintenance and continued improvements.
